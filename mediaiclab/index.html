Biography

I am Chih-Ting Liu (劉致廷 in Chinese), a second-year Ph.D. student at the Media IC and System Lab, National Taiwan University (NTU). I received the B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering (EE) in 2017 and started the M.S. program in Graduate Institute of Electronic Engineering from National Taiwan University. In Feb. 2019, I directly persued my Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Prof. Shao-Yi Chien. Here are my Google Scholar and CV.
